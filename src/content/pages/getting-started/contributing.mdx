Various ways to contribute...

- Just use it and let us know what you think (good or constructive bad).
- Report bugs and suggest improvements.
- Submit a theme to the Shelley project.

## Submitting a theme

Contact us if this is something you are interested in doing. We will provide you with a template to follow and help you through the process.
